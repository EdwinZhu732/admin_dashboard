Classic Medium
Your Adventure Begins... (The Standard Tree Game Experience). As its name suggests, Classic Mode worlds offer Terraria's "normal" gameplay experience which existed prior to the introduction of other world modes. As such, Classic Mode worlds do not feature the "creator" freedom of Journey Mode worlds, but nor are they as challenging as Expert Mode or Master Mode worlds.
